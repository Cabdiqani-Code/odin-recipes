Canbuulo galay

This is a delicious Somali dinner made of red beans and corn

This recipe is call canbuulo

Ingredeints

    Corn
    kedney beans
    Salt
    Sasame oil
    coffee berries

STEPS

    1.Get a 200g of corn
    2.Get a 200g of red beans
    3.Get a pot
    3.Put the pot on the fire and pour 1 litre of water
    4.pour the corn and beans into the warm water
    5.Let them boil for 40 minuts
    6.Now it is ready
TOPPINGS
    1.Get an other pot
    2.Put it on the fire
    3.Pour 100g of sasame oil
    4.Put 50g of coffee berries into the boiling oil
    5.Stir for a minut
    6.Let it cook for 30 minuts
    7.Now dinner is ready to serve
